Being more proactive continues to be a leading goal for security organizations. As AI accelerates the pace of vulnerability discovery and exploitation, organizations will rely on the personalization of their security investments to help prioritize their defenses. To help you be more proactive by matching your real-world exposures with real-time adversary activity, we’ve begun integration efforts between Google Threat Intelligence and Wiz Attack Surface Management (ASM).
