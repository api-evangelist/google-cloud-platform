While multi-region backups offer the highest availability, many organizations want a more cost-effective way to protect their data against a regional outage, but still adhere to data residency requirements. Building on our foundation of multi-region backup protection , we are excited to announce the general availability (GA) of cross-region backups for our Backup and DR Service .
