Protecting sensitive data used with AI is a critical part of our commitment to providing advanced and secure cloud infrastructure. Confidential Computing cryptographically protects data in use in hardware-based Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs) with verifiable data integrity. We are thrilled to share our latest Confidential Computing innovations across our hardware ecosystem that help further strengthen verifiable privacy in cloud AI deployments.
