The automotive industry is at a pivotal crossroads as it hits the gas on adopting new technology. The era of the traditional connected vehicle has shifted into the age of the software-defined vehicle (SDV), notable for rapid innovation with many new capabilities delivered over the air. By integrating AI and agents, the next generation of SDVs will be capable of turning raw telemetry into actionable insights in real-time, allowing for a fundamental rethink of how vehicles interact with their environment and their users .
