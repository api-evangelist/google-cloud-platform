Google Cloud announced the preview of graph algorithms natively integrated with Spanner Graph, bringing Google Research's graph mining capabilities to the database. The algorithms support fraud detection, social network analysis, entity resolution, and healthcare research use cases, with tight integration with ISO Graph Query Language (GQL), near-zero transactional impact, and the ability to run algorithms on graphs with tens of billions of edges within minutes.
